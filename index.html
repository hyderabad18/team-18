About:


The Beginning.. The core team of Youth4Jobs have over a decade of experience in market linked skilling of underprivileged youth. As part of the founding team of EGMM, the AP government Employment mission, they helped change ,for the first time in the country, the tone of the government skilling from trainings for trainings sake to market linked trainings for youth with placement. In six years, they helped make it one of the largest Jobs mission for the poor. The team scaled it with innovations like pro-poor products tailored to market needs - the countrys' first grass-root English, work readiness and Computer (EWRC)Academies and Finishing School. A unique IT architecture linked to e-payment was put in place for transparency . With this rich experience behind them, Youth4Jobs was set up to do pioneering work once again, of training disabled youth and placing them in quality, organized sector jobs.
